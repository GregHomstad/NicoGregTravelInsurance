Common questions

What if my parents have pre-existing medical conditions?

All plans include coverage for pre-existing condition emergencies, from $500 (Ultra Plus) to $6,000 (VIP Plus). This covers unexpected flare-ups and emergencies related to existing conditions. If your parents have chronic conditions like diabetes or heart disease, we recommend VIP or VIP Plus for higher limits.

My parents are over 70. Can they still get coverage?

Yes — we cover travelers up to age 84 on daily plans (5–90 days). Most competitors cap at 70 or 75. For extended stays over 60 days, the age limit is 65.

Can I buy this for my parents from the US?

Absolutely. Most NRI customers do exactly this. You fill in the trip details and pay from the US. Your parents receive the policy documents by email, and you can forward the traveler guide via WhatsApp.

How does "direct hospital payment" work?

When your parents have a medical emergency, they (or you) call our 24/7 assistance center. We coordinate with the hospital and pay the covered amount directly — your parents don't need to put it on a credit card and wait for reimbursement. Pre-authorization is required for non-emergency/planned care.

What happens if something goes wrong after hours or on a weekend?

Our assistance center operates 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. Your parents can call the US toll-free number or WhatsApp us any time. Operators speak Hindi, English, and Spanish.

Is this actual insurance?

TripKavach plans are travel assistance products provided by BMI Travel Assist, a subsidiary of BMI Financial Group. They are not a substitute for health insurance, Medicare, or social security programs. They cover emergencies and unexpected events during international travel.

Can I cancel if plans change?

Yes. If you cancel before the coverage start date (the traveler's departure date), you can receive a full cancellation. Once coverage has started, cancellations are not permitted.

What's NOT covered?

Routine check-ups and wellness visits, elective or cosmetic procedures, pre-planned surgeries, substance abuse treatment, and injuries from extreme sports (unless covered by the amateur sports benefit). Always review the full conditions of coverage before purchasing.

How quickly are claims paid?

For direct hospital payments, we coordinate in real-time during your emergency. For reimbursement claims, our target is resolution within 5-10 business days after receiving complete documentation.
