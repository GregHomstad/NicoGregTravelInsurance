9. Waiting Periods

If the plan is purchased while the beneficiary is already at the destination (outside their country of residence), the following waiting periods apply:

  • 5 days — for all benefits except COVID-19
  • 15 days — for COVID-19 coverage

No waiting period applies when the plan is purchased before departing from the country of residence.

10. Policy Extensions

Extensions are subject to the following conditions:

  • The beneficiary may not request an extension if any services have been used during the current plan period.
  • Extensions must be with equal or higher coverage — downgrading is not permitted.
  • Extension requests must be submitted via info@bmitravelassist.com no more than 8 days before the current plan expires.
  • Extensions are not available if the beneficiary has been abroad for more than 2 years.
  • Any medical condition treated during the original plan is automatically considered pre-existing for the extension period.
  • The extension enters into force immediately after the original plan ends.

11. Exclusions Applicable to All Services and Benefits

The following events and conditions are expressly excluded from all BMI Travel Assist plans:

  1. Pre-existing, chronic, defined, or recurrent conditions existing prior to the plan validity date (whether known to the beneficiary or not), including acute episodes, consequences, and sequelae.
  2. Medical conditions resulting from treatment by professionals not authorized by BMIIC's Assistance Center.
  3. Homeopathic treatments, acupuncture, physical therapy (except accident-related with prior authorization, max 10 sessions), podiatry, thermal cures.
  4. Criminal acts by the beneficiary, directly or indirectly (fights, brawls, etc.).
  5. Conditions caused by intentional drug, narcotic, or alcohol use, or medication taken without a prescription.
  6. Orthopedic devices, prostheses (including dental), eyeglasses, contact lenses, hearing aids, wheelchairs, crutches, durable medical equipment, and implantable devices.
  7. Injuries from sports training, practice, or participation in competitions (professional or amateur). Also excluded: extreme/dangerous sports including motorsports, boxing, polo, water skiing, diving below 15m, hang gliding, karting, ATV, mountaineering, bungee jumping, cycling, caving, skeleton, hunting, bobsleigh, and sports practiced outside regulated facilities.
  8. Childbirth, pregnancy, gynecological exams, and abortion of any kind (unless Futura Mamá upgrade is purchased).
  9. Mental, nervous, or psychological conditions including panic attacks, stress, eating disorders (bulimia, anorexia, etc.).
  10. Conditions related to alcohol consumption of any kind.
  11. HIV/AIDS and all sexually transmitted infections (syphilis, gonorrhea, herpes, chlamydia, HPV, etc.).
  12. Natural disasters: tsunamis, earthquakes, storms, hurricanes, floods, nuclear radiation, and any event classified as a national, regional, or local disaster.
  13. Suicide, attempted suicide, or self-inflicted injuries.
  14. War, invasion, terrorism, hostilities, civil unrest, rebellion, military operations, riots.
  15. Bad faith or intentional misrepresentation by the beneficiary.
  16. Routine medical exams, diagnostic check-ups, and lab tests to determine if a condition is pre-existing.
  17. Transport costs (public or private) to medical facilities, unless expressly authorized by the Assistance Center.
  18. Congenital conditions or diseases derived from congenital deformities.
  19. Injuries from private (non-commercial) aircraft, including charter flights.
  20. Injuries from fights or brawls (unless verified legitimate self-defense with police report), strikes, vandalism, or riots in which the beneficiary actively participated.
  21. Endemic, pandemic, or epidemic diseases if the beneficiary did not follow travel restrictions, prophylactic treatment, or vaccination recommendations.
  22. Any medical expense not previously authorized by the BMIIC Assistance Center.
  23. Menstrual disorders and related conditions in women.
  24. Liver diseases (cirrhosis, abscesses, etc.).
  25. Stress tests and preventive check-up hospitalizations.
  26. Hernias of any type and their consequences.
  27. Kidnapping or attempted kidnapping.
  28. Occupational hazards: injuries/illness from professional tasks involving specialized labor, hazardous substances, heavy machinery, or special physical skills.
  29. Injuries to drivers or passengers of any vehicle (including bicycles and motorcycles) without a valid license, helmet, or insurance.
  30. Assistance during active armed conflict, civil disturbances, or threatening situations in the beneficiary's location.

12. Excluded Countries

No coverage available: Iraq, Ethiopia, Syria, Ukraine, Yemen, Sudan, Somalia, North Korea, Israel, Afghanistan, and Cuba.

Limited coverage (subject to sanctions and special licenses): Venezuela, Russia, and Belarus.

This list is subject to change. Visit bmitravelassist.com for updates.

13. Additional Conditions

  • No coverage for beneficiaries in illegal immigration or work status.
  • COVID-19 coverage requires complete WHO-approved vaccination proof.
  • Events during any waiting period are not covered during or after the waiting period.
  • If the purpose of travel is to receive medical treatment abroad for a pre-existing condition, coverage will be denied.
  • All claims must be filed within 30 calendar days of voucher expiry. After this deadline, no claims will be accepted.
  • Any disputes shall be submitted to the jurisdiction of the courts of the State of Florida, USA.
