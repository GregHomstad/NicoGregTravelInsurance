Student Plan FAQs

Our student plan is available to international students holding F-1 (academic student), J-1 (exchange visitor), M-1 (vocational student), and similar student visa types. Students on OPT or CPT extensions are also eligible. Contact us if you have a different visa type and we will help determine your eligibility.

Yes, our student plan can be aligned with your academic calendar. You can purchase coverage for a single semester, a full academic year, or even extend through summer sessions and breaks. Coverage duration is flexible to match your study period in the United States.

Our student plan covers injuries from recreational and intramural sports activities. If you participate in intercollegiate or competitive athletics, additional coverage may be required.
