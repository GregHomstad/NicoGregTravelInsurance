Annual Multi-trip Type

Calendar year coverage

For frequent business or pleasure travelers. Covers all trips during one calendar year — each trip up to 30, 45, 60, or 90 consecutive days depending on the plan selected.

If your parents visit the US multiple times a year, one annual policy covers every trip — no need to buy separate coverage each time.
