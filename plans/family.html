Family Plan FAQs

Our family plan covers members of all ages, from infants to seniors. Each family member receives the same comprehensive coverage regardless of age. Pricing may vary based on the age of each individual member.

There is no strict limit on the number of family members who can be included under a single family policy. You can add parents, children, grandparents, and other immediate family members. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your family size.

Yes, our family plan offers group discounts when multiple family members are enrolled under one policy. The more members you add, the greater the savings compared to purchasing individual plans. Get a quote to see your family's specific pricing.
