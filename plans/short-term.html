Eligibility

Who is eligible?

  • Indian travelers visiting the United States
  • Trips lasting 5 to 90 consecutive days
  • Ages up to 74 (regular premium)
  • Ages 75-84 accepted at 3x premium
  • Any form of international travel (air, cruise, land)

Perfect for

  • Vacation travelers visiting the US
  • Parents visiting children in the US
  • Short business trips
  • Cruise passengers with US stops

Short-term Plan FAQs

Yes, you can apply for coverage before or after you arrive in the United States. However, we recommend purchasing before your trip begins for continuous coverage from day one.

If your trip exceeds 90 days, consider our Long-term plan (60-365 days) which is designed for extended stays. You may also contact our support team about extending your current coverage.

With our direct billing feature, we pay the healthcare provider directly, so you don't have to pay out of pocket and wait for reimbursement. Simply contact our 24/7 assistance team and they will coordinate with the provider.

Our short-term plans are available to travelers of all ages. Pricing may vary based on age group. Get a personalized quote to see exact rates for your situation.
