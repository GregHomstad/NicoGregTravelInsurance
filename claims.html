Filing a Claim

We make the claims process simple and stress-free.

How It Works

1

Contact Us

Reach our 24/7 assistance team by phone or email immediately after an incident.

2

Gather Documents

Collect required documentation including medical bills, receipts, and reports.

3

Submit Your Claim

Send your claim with supporting documents to our dedicated claims team.

4

Receive Payment

Claims reviewed within 10-15 business days. Direct payment or reimbursement processed.

Required Documents

  • Itemized medical bills
  • Doctor's report / diagnosis
  • Police report (if accident)
  • Copy of passport / travel documents
  • Proof of travel dates
  • Receipts for expenses

Important Tips

File Claims Promptly

File claims as soon as possible after an incident to ensure timely processing.

Keep Copies of Everything

Keep copies of all documents submitted for your records.

Get Pre-Authorization

Contact our team before seeking non-emergency treatment for pre-authorization.
