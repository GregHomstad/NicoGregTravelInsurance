Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, phone number, and travel details when you request a quote, use our Plan Finder, or contact us.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process your insurance plan inquiries
  • Communicate with you about your coverage
  • Send relevant updates and information
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We share information with BMI Travel Assist, our insurance partner, solely for the purpose of processing your insurance application and claims. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, though some features may not function properly.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us at support@tripkavach.com to exercise these rights.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
