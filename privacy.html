Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Victoria Advisory Group LLC, a limited liability company organized under the laws of the State of Wyoming, United States, operating as TripKavach (“TripKavach,” “we,” “our,” or “us”), collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you use our website at tripkavach.com (the “Site”).

This Privacy Policy is incorporated into and forms part of our Terms and Conditions. By using the Site, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, you must not use the Site.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender, and passport number, as provided during the enrollment process or when contacting us.

Travel Information: Departure country, destination, travel start and end dates, and number of travelers.

Payment Information: Credit card number, cardholder name, expiration date, and CVV. This data is transmitted directly to BEST MERIDIAN INTERNATIONAL INSURANCE COMPANY I.I. (“BMIIC”) via a secure, SSL-encrypted tunnel and is never stored on our servers.

Technical Information: IP address, browser type and version, device information, and user agent string, collected automatically when you access the Site.

Consent Records: When you agree to our Terms and Conditions during enrollment, we record the version of the Terms you agreed to, the date and time of your agreement, your email address, and your enrollment reference ID for legal compliance purposes.

3. How We Collect Information

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Directly from you: When you use the quote generator, Plan Finder, or contact form, or when you communicate with us by email or phone.
  • Automatically: When you visit the Site, we automatically collect technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and device information through server logs.
  • From third parties: BMIIC may share information with us in connection with the administration of your travel assistance membership.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate enrollment in travel assistance memberships issued by BMIIC.
  • To communicate with you regarding your membership, including via text message, email, and phone call, which may include automated messages. Message and data rates may apply.
  • To facilitate the processing of assistance requests on your behalf with BMIIC.
  • To record and maintain proof of your consent to our Terms and Conditions for legal compliance.
  • To improve the functionality, performance, and content of the Site.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations, including responding to lawful requests from public authorities.

5. Information Sharing

We share your information with the following parties and for the following purposes:

BMIIC (Travel Assist Provider): We share your personal information, travel information, and payment information with BMIIC to process your enrollment, administer your membership, and facilitate assistance requests. As described in our Terms and Conditions, you authorize TripKavach, BMIIC, and any authorized partners to release and exchange information necessary to fulfill membership purposes, including medical records, hospital records, membership records, treatment history, and related documentation.

Service Providers: We use third-party service providers for website hosting (Vercel Inc.), database services (Supabase Inc.), and related infrastructure. These providers process data on our behalf and are contractually obligated to protect your information.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, subpoena, or other legal process, or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Payment card data is transmitted through a secure, SSL-encrypted tunnel directly to BMIIC and is never stored on our servers. Consent records and other data stored in our database are encrypted at rest.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for the following periods:

  • Consent records: Retained indefinitely for legal audit and compliance purposes.
  • Membership-related data: Retained for the duration of your membership and any period required by applicable law or contractual obligation.
  • Contact form submissions: Retained for up to two (2) years from the date of submission.
  • Technical logs: Retained for up to ninety (90) days.

8. Cookies and Third-Party Services

The Site uses a Google Translate widget to provide translation services. This widget may set cookies on your device and transmit data to Google’s servers. Google’s use of this data is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy.

You can configure your browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. If you disable cookies, some features of the Site, including the translation widget, may not function properly.

9. International Data Transfers

TripKavach is operated from the United States. If you access the Site from India or any other country outside the United States, please be aware that your information will be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those of your country of residence. By using the Site, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to our legal and contractual retention obligations.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications. However, as stated in our Terms and Conditions, communications related to the administration and servicing of your membership are essential and may not be subject to an opt-out.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

11. Children’s Privacy

The Site and our services are available only to users who are 18 years of age or older, as stated in our Terms and Conditions. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18 years of age. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a minor, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

12. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, TripKavach will notify you and any applicable regulatory authorities in accordance with applicable data protection laws. TripKavach’s liability for any data breach shall be limited to the extent permitted by applicable law and shall not exceed the liability cap set forth in Section 13 of our Terms and Conditions.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time by posting the revised version on this page. Any changes become effective upon posting. Your continued use of the Site after any such changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy. You should review this page periodically for updates.
