Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our travel insurance plans.

Eligibility

Non-US residents and citizens traveling to the United States seeking temporary coverage are eligible for our plans. Coverage is available for individuals, families, students, and corporate groups.

Age restrictions vary by plan. Short-term and annual plans cover all ages. Long-term plans are available for travelers up to age 65.

Yes, you can apply for coverage before or during your trip. We recommend purchasing as early as possible to ensure continuous coverage throughout your stay.

Coverage is independent of visa status. Regardless of the type of visa you hold, you are eligible for our travel insurance plans.

Coverage

Yes, our plans include coverage for pre-existing conditions, including acute onset of pre-existing conditions. Coverage limits vary by plan.

Our plans cover hospitalization, outpatient visits, specialist consultations, emergency dental, medical evacuation, repatriation of remains, and accidental death & dismemberment (AD&D).

Exclusions include elective procedures, cosmetic surgery, routine checkups, and self-inflicted injuries. Please review your plan documents for a complete list of exclusions.

Coverage limits vary by plan. Get a personalized quote for specific limits based on your travel needs.

Claims

Contact our 24/7 assistance team to initiate the claims process. Our dedicated claims specialists will guide you through every step and help with all required documentation.

Claims are typically processed within 10–15 business days after all required documentation has been received. Our team works to resolve claims as quickly as possible.

With direct billing, we pay the healthcare provider directly so you have no out-of-pocket costs at the time of service. Simply present your insurance information at a network provider.

You will typically need medical bills, a doctor's report, a copy of your passport, and proof of travel. Our claims team will provide a complete checklist when you initiate your claim.

General

Yes, policies can be extended. Contact our support team before your current coverage expires to request an extension.

Yes, we offer a pro-rated refund if your policy is cancelled before the coverage start date. Contact our support team for details on the refund process.
