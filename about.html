About TripKavach

We provide comprehensive, affordable travel insurance for Indian travelers visiting the United States — so you can travel with complete peace of mind.

Our Mission

TripKavach was created to serve the Indian traveler community with travel insurance that is easy, transparent, and genuinely protective. In partnership with BMI Travel Assist — a subsidiary of BMI Financial Group with over 50 years of experience — we bring world-class coverage to Indian travelers visiting the United States.

We believe that your health history shouldn't prevent you from traveling. That's why our plans cover pre-existing conditions, charge zero deductibles, and pay healthcare providers directly — so you never face reimbursement hassles abroad.

We serve the Indian diaspora — families visiting loved ones, IT professionals on assignments, students pursuing higher education, and tourists exploring the US. Our own 24/7 Assistance Center ensures quality control at every step.

Whether you're visiting family, studying abroad, or on a business trip, we have a plan designed for you.

What We Offer

Multiple Plan Types

Short-term, long-term, annual, family, corporate, and student plans tailored to your trip.

Pre-existing Coverage

Your health history doesn't limit your travel. Our plans cover pre-existing medical conditions.

24/7 Support

Round-the-clock global assistance and dedicated claims support whenever you need it.

Powered by BMI Travel Assist

TripKavach partners with BMI Travel Assist, a subsidiary of BMI Financial Group with over 50 years of industry experience. Headquartered at the BMI Building, Town Center One, 8950 SW 74th Court, Miami, FL 33156, BMI operates one of the largest worldwide traveler assistance networks.
